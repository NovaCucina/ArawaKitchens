About ArawaKitchens

ArawaKitchens is dedicated to bringing premium European kitchen solutions to the Guyanese market. Our focus is on clean design, durable materials, and customer-centered customization. We proudly work with top European kitchen brands known for quality and innovation.

We're Launching Soon!

We are excited to announce that Arawa Kitchens will open its first kitchen showroom in Georgetown, Guyana. We are preparing a curated kitchen collection tailored to the needs and style of Guyanese homeowners and developers, showcasing only top-tier brands from Europe.

Our Services

In addition to supplying premium kitchens for private homes, ArawaKitchens also provides solutions for:

  • Residential construction and housing projects
  • Turnkey kitchen packages for developers
  • Customized kitchens for commercial and corporate environments
  • Consultancy and kitchen planning for building professionals

We support architects, contractors, and businesses in delivering beautiful, functional kitchen spaces for every type of project.
